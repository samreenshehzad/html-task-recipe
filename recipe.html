Chikoo Milk shake(by chef Zakir):Recipe

Ingredients

  • 250 grams chikoo

  • 3 cups milk

  • 6 tbsp sugar

  • 4 tbsp beaten cream

  • 1/4 cup crushed ice



Method


  • Blend chicos,milk,sugar and 2 tbsp cream in a blender.

  • When all things mixed well then add ice and blend again.

  • Pour it in a glass and put whipped cream on top.
